Ingredients

  • 6 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup milk , or a little more if needed
  • 1/3 cup chocolate sauce
  • 12 oreos
  • whipped cream (optional for topping)

Directions

Step 1
Gather all ingredients.

Step 2
Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate sauce, and Oreos in a blender and blend until
combined. Top with whipped cream.

Step 3
If you like a smooth shake blend all the ingredients at the same time. If you like your Oreo
milkshake chunkier, either blend the Oreos briefly towards the end of mixing or crush
them by hand and stir them in after all ingredients have blended.
